Arlandia Classes

In the Universe of Arlandia, the classic classes have been rebalanced to give the weak classes more interest. This is done by uppering the experience points got by the weak classes and lowering the experience points got by the strong classes, like the following diagram :
 

Classes XPs Other bonuses/maluses
Barbarian 100%  
Bard 140%  
Cleric 60%  
Druid 120%  
Fighter 80%  
Monk 120%  
Paladin 100%  
Ranger 120% automatic "Cartographer" and "Leader" talents
Rogue 120%  
Sorcerer 80% spells "Continual Flame", "Darkflame" and "Flame Weapon" forbidden
Wizard 80% spells "Continual Flame", "Darkflame" and "Flame Weapon" forbidden


Other bonuses/maluses may be added in the future.
