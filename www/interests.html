Arlandia Interests

Here is a short description of all the interests you can find on the planets :

 

Cities

Cities are the first civilised places in the Universe. They hold most of the facilities needed by starting players, like shops, transports, plots, take off places and other conveniances.

Each planet holds one city or base. The base is just a Starport built on a small or hostile planet, or a moon. The base only contains a few of the city facilities.

The sewers of the cities can be a good start for training new characters.

icon :

 

 

Towns

Beside of cities, the towns are the second civilised places in the Universe. They are generated randomly by the system and holds most of the city facilities, like shops, transports, plots and a resting place.

The airships in town come 3 times during the day, check the schedule. Starships do not come to towns, but players who got an own starship can take off from towns anyway.

icon :

 

 

Dungeons

As on any server, dungeons are the most important interest place for players or for DM adventures. There are many different types of dungeons in UOA, going from a simple crypt to the huge castle through ruins, towers, temples, etc.

The content of the dungeons is chosen randomly according to the planet settings, and many surprises can be generated inside, much more than just killing creatures. Be prepared to solve enigms to go farther in your dungeon quest.

Creatures, resources, treasures, enigms, everything is generated randomly and change quite rarely. And the website shows LIVE with is happening in each dungeon.

Take care of the wandering monsters, they go out and in the dungeons, and move from one level to the others !!!

icon :

 

 

Castles

Castles are the third civilised places in the whole Universe, but they also contains evil hidden places, abandonned since a long time. So visiting a Lord can be helpful for some shopping but in the cellars of the castle huge beasts are waiting for you.

Castles holds a few shops, a plotgiver, but no transport places.

icon :

 

 

Ruins

Ruins are old cities, castles or domains destroyed since a long time. Only a few walls remain from the magnificent structure. By searching carefully, some treasures or even entries to lost places my be found.

icon :

 

 

Animal reserves

On some planets, people hunt the local animal life and bring the animal to a reserve, where they are protected from predators and raised for the needed of the civilised races.

Everyone can go to an animal reserve and buy animals to have food or other animal resources. It is sometimes better than loosing to much time hunting by itself.

The entry to an animal reserve may require money, an enigm resolution or a fight. Once inside all the animals have to be bought.

icon :

 

 

Resource mountains

A resource mountain contains 10 times more resources than any other area on the planet. So it is a privileged place for anybody who need resources in huge quantities.

Like for the animal reserve, an entry has to be paid, with money, an enigm resolution or a fight. Once inside the resources have to be cut.

At the resource mountains, resources regenerate infinitely.

icon :

 

 

Amusement places

Everybody sometimes need to relax and to enjoy life in its light ways. That's why some group of people decided to build entertainment places where everybody can come and take benefit from the offered amusement possibilities, like tournaments, enigm resolutions, fake fights, shows, etc.

Being successful at amusement places can temporary raise some characteristics.

icon :

 

 

Domains

Domains are the most interesting interest for players because they are their economic place. Players can build them in any free area and developp a real economic activity.

Visitors can take benefit from all the structures built buy the holder.

Visit the Domains section to learn more about the domains.
