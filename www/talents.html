Arlandia Talents

In addition to the basic skills and feats, players can also learn extra talents. Talents can be learned at any level and is accessible to every race and class. Here is a list of the available talents :

 

Talent

Description
Cartographer Let the players know their position. Rangers get this talent for free and automatically.
Clone Let the players clone themselves as permanent henchmen.
Horse Horde When mounting your horse, all your humanoid henchs will also mount a horse and get its bonuses.
Item Sensor Let the players know where the nearest laying-on-the-ground item is located.
Leader Let the players know where their henchs are.
Super Power Give the players temporary bonuses. This talent costs a few xps for each use.
